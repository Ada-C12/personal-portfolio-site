HOBBIES

Travel

As much as I love to stay home for free, when I do get to travel I keep it action-packed. Two of the pictures above are taken in this lovely German town called Miltenberg, and their buildings are the stuff of fairytales. I appreciate all the beauty that is in this world, from natural sceneries to architectural marvels, from breathtaking art to the warmth of simple country folks... Travelling the world really is one of the best ways to get a sense of history and to connect with other humans.

I mean... who doesn't like to eat? It's one of the funnest things ever, also, it keeps you from dying. I shouldn't have to write a whole article to tell you about the merits of nutrients and ingestion. Next!

Movies

Ok, now we're talking. I mean... who doesn't like to watch movies? I got some recommendations for y'all! Mostly horror though because that is my jam! The most perfect and truly scary horror movie ever? I think it's a 3-way tie with the Shining, 28 days later, and Hereditary. How about something lighter, you say? Well I got you fam! I heartily recommend this movie called Idiocracy, it came out like 10+ years ago, and has largely flown under the radar. It is immensely quotable and absurdly prophetic for the times we live in. Oh, you don't like to laugh? That's cool, I got something else in the van here for you... how about a rocking musical with an achingly beautiful story and equally amazing songs? Go check out Hedwig and the Angry Inch!

Knitting

Check this out, I've been knitting for 10+ years I think. I first started it because I needed a portable hobby to kill time with, and it just really spiraled out of my control. Besides generating totally useful end products, the process of knitting is therapeutic for pent-up anxiety, AND squishing yarn balls is literal joy. After several years of collecting yarn and various fibers (I spin yarn too haha), the size of my stash is quite obscene... I don't even want to tell you how much I have, lol. See the picture below with the matching sweaters my husband and I wore in front of a windmill in Amsterdam? I made those!

Krav Maga

I don't like exercise, straight up. However, it is perfectly tolerable if I'm learning something while I exercise. I grew up on kung fu movies, and like every well-adjusted kid, I fantasize about being a vigilante crime fighter who beat up bad people AND look hella good doing it. So 5 years ago I decided to do something about it and actually checked out a krav maga gym. Fell in love and haven't looked back since. I also dabble in Philipino Martial Arts (FMA) and Brazilian Jujitsu. Below is a picture of me with a grandmaster of one of the FMA disciplines called Petit Tirsia Kali, he signed my knife lol.

Perfumes

Shoot, even I'm surprised by how much of a frag-head I am. But hear me out, you know how some smells can trigger certain memories, or put you in a different mood? Yeah, I know you'd understand, high five!
