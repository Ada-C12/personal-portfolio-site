ABOUT ME

Ok, for reals though, who am I?

I'm a fun-loving individual who plays hard but also works hard.

Some may say that I'm real "extra", and yeah I'm going to have to agree with that.

Because what point is living if you're just going to do it half-assed?

I didn't have a whole lot growing up, but I had curiosity and drive.

And thank goodness for that, because my life may have turned out quite terribly...

Why? Well, after I was born, my parents took me to see a fortune teller, this was in Taiwan and it was a different time and culture.

Anyway, this guy told my parents that I was going to grow up to be a loser, and they believed him.

I can hear you screaming and I'm with you 100%, he's a jackass.

I wasn't going to let this define me though... I happened to like school and yeah, I wanted to make a better life for myself too.

I worked hard both in school and also on my after-school assembly line job.

I knew that if I wanted something in life, I had to go out there and make it happen for myself.

Despite early setbacks, I'm aware of how lucky I am and I'm grateful for the many opportunities that have come my way.
