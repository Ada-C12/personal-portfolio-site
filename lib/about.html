About Me

I live in lovely Seattle and am a student at Ada Developers Academy (shout out to my fellow Adies and cohort 12 peeps!).

Before the mid-career pivot to programming, I spent 11 years in hospital operations (think reports and documentation). I learned that while configuring a piece of software can be satisfying, writing my own code is what I'm really passionate about. The power! The responsibility!

Past lives include newspaper reporter in small-town Alaska, customer service representative at an e-commerce startup, and, like approximately 95 percent of my fellow Seattlelites, barista.

Back when I had spare time, I played ukulele for my cat and bassoon with several community groups, including the Fremont Philharmonic. I also did lots of cooking, reading, and binging on British TV shoes, and a little hiking and gardening.
