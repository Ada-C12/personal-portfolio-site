About

Hi, my name is Dianna Tingg.

I'm currently a full-stack software engineering student at Ada Developers Academy in Seattle, Washington. I will graduate in June 2020.

Ada is an intensive training program for women and gender diverse people who want to become software developers. It includes six months of full-time classroom instruction followed by five months in a paid industry internship.

The curriculum includes Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Git. We are also learning about computer science fundamentals, test-driven development, pair programming, leadership principles, social justice, and inclusion.

Before Ada, I was working in the healthcare industry as a Quality Program Manager. I specialized in Medicare regulations and electronic medical record systems.

In my spare time, I like to write business reviews. I've been a member of the Yelp Elite Squad for two years. Check out my blog for some of my reviews.
