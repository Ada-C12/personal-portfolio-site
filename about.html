About

I'm learning to code at the Ada Academy, a school aiming to "diversify tech by providing women and gender diverse people the skills, experience, and community support to become professional software developers." Each day I try to channel 1/1000 of the swagger of our inspiration, Ada Lovelace.

I'm approaching programming with a musician's perspective, having spent the first chapter of professional life writing, analyzing, teaching and playing music. I've interned at Bedroom Community Records, directed music at Holden Village, and served as Seattle's Groupmuse Community Coordinator. I'm excited to explore every parallel I find between music and tech.
