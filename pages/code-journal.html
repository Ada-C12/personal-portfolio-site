My Code Journey

My coding journey began last year when I served as the STEM Enrichment Coordinator of a college-readiness program for high school students. As part of my job, I coordinated a weekly Girls Who Code club for my students. At first, I was terrified to lead club activities because I didn't have any knowledge or experience with coding. However, as I began completing online tutorials and teaching myself the basics of Python, I became more and more excited about the new possibilities available to me.

One afternoon, I stumbled upon the website for Ada Developer's Academy while researching free coding resources in Seattle. At the time, Ada seemed like a far-off dream, but somehow less than a year later it has become my reality. I began my journey at Ada in August 2019, and I am currently completing the classroom portion of the program.

In February 2020, I will begin a software development internship at a tech company in Seattle where I will work for 5 months before formally launching my programming career.

I am countinuously surprised by the opportunities that are opening up for me as a software developer, and I am looking forward to seeing where this journey takes me next!
