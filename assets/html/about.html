About Me

I began my coding journey years ago, back in 2009 when I took an intro to Python course. I learned html in the years to follow and learned MySQL in 2015. Despite all of this, I did not think I was fit to be a developer. Until in 2017 when I dabbled in more html, css, javascript, and other web development skills. I realized this field was a good fit for me and I enjoyed it. I applied to Ada Developers Acaademy in the start of 2019 and was accepted to Cohort 12. My background is in International Studies (politics) and worked in administration and finance before coming to Ada.
