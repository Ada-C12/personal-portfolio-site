Lost Diver

A vertical scroller game made in Unity for Android with a simple directive: go up

Lost Diver is my first try at making a Unity game, and it's programmed in C#. My sweetheart Trevor Davies acted as chief creative designer, and as my first pair programming partner. The controls only allow the player to move the free Unity asset art diver left or right as you try to get them as close to the surface as possible (currently impossible, sorry - it's a work in (slow) progress) while avoiding an increasing density of mines and collecting bubbles of extra air. Current state downloadable for Android: APK file.

We started this project by loosely following some of Charger Games' youtube tutorials for "Unity Racing Game"
